Decrypt the Code

The Caesar cipher is one of the simplest and earliest known substitution ciphers, where each letter in the plaintext is shifted a certain number of places down or up the alphabet. This technique was named after Julius Caesar, who supposedly used it to communicate confidential information.

HOW TO PLAY
